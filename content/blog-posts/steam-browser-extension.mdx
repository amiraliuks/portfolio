## What is this & why I made it
I’ve been using Steam for years, and there’s one thing that’s always been missing.
Kosovo just isn’t listed on community profiles. It’s a small thing, sure. But if you’re actually from here, it doesn’t feel small. It just feels weird not being there at all. People have been pointing this out forever. Nothing changed.

So instead of waiting, I just made a fix myself.

It’s a simple browser extension that replaces the missing flag with the correct Kosovo one. That’s it. No extra stuff, it just fixes the problem.

I like Steam, I really do. But this is something they could fix in seconds, and it would mean a lot to a lot of people.
