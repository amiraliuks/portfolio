# Solving

After opening the web instance, there were 3 questions that needed to be answered:
1. Sharri is one of Kosova's mountain ranges. What is the Albanian word for the eagle on Kosova's flag, also the national symbol?
	1. **Shqiponja** was the correct answer.
2. What is the name of the iconic 2008 monument in central Prishtina, made of letters that spell an English word celebrating the declaration?
	1. **NEWBORN** was the correct answer.
3. I forgot most of the question but it basically asked when Kosovo got its independence.
	1. **2008** was the correct answer.
